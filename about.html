Who We Are

Crafting Digital Masterpieces Since 2019

AppStello Labs is a full-service digital agency based in Dhanbad, Jharkhand, India. Founded in 2019, we have grown from a small startup into a trusted technology partner for businesses across India and beyond.

We specialize in creating powerful, scalable, and user-friendly digital solutions — from stunning websites and mobile apps to comprehensive digital marketing campaigns. Our approach combines technical expertise with creative thinking to deliver results that truly matter.

  • 200+ successful projects delivered across 15+ industries
  • Team of 25+ certified developers, designers, and marketers
  • Clients across India, UAE, UK, USA, and Southeast Asia
  • Award-winning designs recognized by global design communities
  • 98% client satisfaction rate with dedicated post-launch support

Our Mission

To empower businesses of all sizes with innovative, affordable, and high-quality digital solutions. We believe every business deserves a powerful online presence, and our mission is to make that accessible — from the smallest startup to the largest enterprise.

We strive to deliver projects that not only meet but exceed expectations, using the latest technologies and best practices to create digital experiences that drive real business growth.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and innovative digital agency, known for transforming businesses through technology. We envision a future where every business, regardless of size or location, can compete in the digital world with confidence.

By 2028, we aim to be the go-to technology partner for 1,000+ businesses globally, with offices across India and a reputation for excellence that extends worldwide.
