Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Depending on complexity, a basic website takes 5–10 days, while a full e-commerce platform can take 3–6 weeks. We'll give you an exact timeline during consultation.

What is included in the free 1-year service?

Our free 1-year service includes bug fixes, minor content updates, security patches, and technical support. Major feature additions are billed separately.

Do you work with clients outside India?

Absolutely! We work with clients from India, UAE, UK, USA, and Southeast Asia. We're fully equipped for remote collaboration and communication.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, bank transfer (NEFT/IMPS/RTGS), PayPal, and card payments. We typically work with a 50% advance and 50% on project completion.
